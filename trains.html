Reservation Process

Follow these simple steps to make a reservation:

  1. Select your preferred train from the dropdown menu.
  2. Choose the date of your journey.
  3. Enter your name and select your desired seat.
  4. Click the "Reserve Now" button to confirm your reservation.

Once reserved, you will receive a confirmation email with your ticket details.
